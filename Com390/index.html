Haag Designs

Started in 2013, Haag Designs has continued to lead the world in stunning visuals and powerful visual effects. Josh Haag, founder and operator has been featured in GQ and Forbes magazine as the "Sexiest Designer Alive" for 2014.

My name is Josh Haag. I am a senior at Fresno Pacific University and my area of study is Graphic Design. I enjoy making concert posters for bands that I like and bands that my friends like as well. Digital Illustration is a passion of mine and I like using Adobe Illustrator to make my ideas come to life!

This poster was designed for my "Exploring Color" class. This final project took about 8 hours of work to make this 24"x36" poster of Breaking Bad.

This is a poster I designed in my free time. It is supposed to be able to be used as a poster promoting the band "Deftones" and an upcoming concert that could be having.
